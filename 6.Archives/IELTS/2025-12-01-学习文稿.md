## 2. 思维模型 (Thinking Models)

- **第一性原理 (First Principles)**: 拆解至物理底层逻辑，拒绝经验类比。
- **效用曲线 (Utility Curve)**: `价值 = 覆盖人数 * 有用性`。
- **Ego-能力比 (Ego/Ability)**: 比值 > 1 导致决策脱离现实；需内化责任，击碎 ego。
- **极限思维 (Thinking in the Limit)**: 推演变量至最大/最小，识别长期趋势。
- **多路径冗余**: 构建多个替代方案 (如 5-10 个 AI 并存) 降低系统性风险。

## 3. 商业隐喻 (Business Metaphors)

- **Wings Clipped**: 企业因外部干预 (如股东) 失去战略自主权。
- **Chips on the Table**: 获利后不离场，全额复投至更具潜力的新项目。
- **Tiny Candle**: 宇宙黑暗中的人类意识，极其稀缺且脆弱。
- **Great Filter**: 阻碍生命成为跨星际文明的关键障碍 (如核战、AI 失控)。

## 4. 雅思口语/写作模版 (IELTS Templates)

### 科技类 (Technology)
- **论点**: `[Tech] is in its early stages and will profoundly reshape human society.`
- **案例**: `SpaceX reduced costs by 70% via reusable technology, laying the foundation for commercial space.`
- **伦理**: `Breakthroughs should balance commercial interests with ethics and safety.`

### 职业/创业类 (Career)
- **核心**: `Goal: solving practical problems > pursuing high valuations.`
- **实践**: `Hands-on practice > empty talk about theories.`
- **策略**: `Focus on core contradictions and make efficient decisions in crises.`

### 文明类 (Civilization)
- **存续**: `Becoming multiplanetary is a "backup plan" for human civilization.`
- **共生**: `Pursue human-machine collaborative evolution rather than replacement.`

## 5. 关键实操 (Key Implementation)

- **xAI**: 复用废弃工厂 + 租用冷却设备 = 高效部署超算集群。
