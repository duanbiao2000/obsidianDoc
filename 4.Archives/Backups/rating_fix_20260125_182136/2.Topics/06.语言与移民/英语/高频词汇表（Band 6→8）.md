# 高频词汇表（Band 6→8升级）

> 💡 **核心理念**：词汇升级不是简单替换，而是精确性表达（Precision）。

## 🎯 分类词汇升级表

### 一、表达观点（Expressing Opinions）

#### Band 6 → Band 7.5 → Band 8

| Band 6 | Band 7 | Band 8 | 使用场景 | 例句 |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **think** | **believe** | **argue/contend/maintain** | Academic writing/IELTS Part 3 | "I **argue** that..." / "Critics **contend** that..." |
| **I feel** | **In my opinion** | **From my standpoint/perspective** | Formal writing | "**From my standpoint**, the benefits outweigh..." |
| **I agree** | **I concur** | **I am in alignment with** | Agreement | "I **concur** with the view that..." |
| **maybe** | **perhaps** | **it is plausible that** | Speculation | "**It is plausible that**..." |
| **surely** | **certainly** | **undoubtedly/indisputably** | Emphasis | "This is **undisputably** the case..." |

#### 精确表达训练

**错误示例**（Band 6）：
> I **think** social media is **bad** for teenagers.

**升级版本**（Band 7）：
> I **believe** social media has **detrimental** effects on teenagers.

**高级版本**（Band 8）：
> I **would argue** that social media can be **detrimental** to teenagers' mental wellbeing, particularly when used **excessively**.

---

### 二、展示证据（Presenting Evidence）

#### Band 6 → Band 7.5 → Band 8

| Band 6 | Band 7 | Band 8 | 使用场景 | 例句 |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **show** | **demonstrate** | **exemplify/corroborate** | Academic argument | "This **exemplifies** the trend..." |
| **prove** | **indicate** | **substantiate/validate** | Research support | "Studies **substantiate** this claim..." |
| **for example** | **for instance** | **a case in point** | Examples | "Finland is **a case in point**..." |
| **you can see** | **evidently** | **it is evident that** | Observation | "**It is evident that**..." |
| **many people** | **numerous individuals** | **a substantial proportion** | Quantification | "**A substantial proportion** of..." |

#### 精确表达训练

**错误示例**（Band 6）：
> The study **shows** that homework **helps** students learn better.

**升级版本**（Band 7）：
> Research **demonstrates** that homework **facilitates** learning.

**高级版本**（Band 8）：
> Empirical studies **consistently demonstrate** that well-designed homework **significantly enhances** learning outcomes.

### 三、解决问题（Addressing Problems）

#### Band 6 → Band 7.5 → Band 8

| Band 6 | Band 7 | Band 8 | 使用场景 | 例句 |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **solve** | **address** | **tackle/mitigate** | Problem-solving | "We must **tackle** this issue..." |
| **fix** | **remedy** | **rectify/alleviate** | Correction | "Policies can **alleviate**..." |
| **stop** | **prevent** | **curb/preclude** | Prevention | "Measures to **curb**..." |
| **make better** | **improve** | **enhance/optimize** | Enhancement | "**Optimize** the system..." |
| **answer** | **solution** | **remedy/resolution** | Solutions | "Propose a **viable remedy**..." |

#### 精确表达训练

**错误示例**（Band 6）：
> We need to **solve** the pollution problem.

**升级版本**（Band 7）：
> We need to **address** the environmental **degradation**.

**高级版本**（Band 8）：
> We must **implement comprehensive measures** to **mitigate** environmental **degradation** and **promote sustainability**.

### 四、强调重要（Emphasizing Importance）

| **very bad** | **terrible** | **catastrophic/disastrous** | Strong criticism | "**Catastrophic** consequences..." |
| **big problem** | **serious issue** | **pressing concern** | Urgency | "A **pressing concern**..." |

## 🛠️ 学习与练习方法

### 方法1：替换练习（Substitution Drill）

**步骤**：
1. 选择一段Band 6的句子（如之前的示例）
2. 逐词替换为Band 7，再替换为Band 8
3. 对比三个版本，体会精确性差异

**示例**：
> Social media **makes** teenagers **feel** **bad**.
>
> ↓ Band 7
> Social media **causes** teenagers to **experience** **negative emotions**.
>
> ↓ Band 8
> Social media can **precipitate** feelings of **inadequacy** and **anxiety** among adolescents.

### 方法2：造句练习（Sentence Building）

**步骤**：
1. 选择5个Band 8词汇
2. 为每个词汇造3个不同的句子
3. 应用到不同话题（教育/科技/环保等）

**模板**：
```markdown
词汇：exemplify

句1（教育）：
Finland's education system **exemplifies** how reducing homework can improve outcomes.

句2（科技）：
Smartphones **exemplify** the double-edged nature of technological progress.

句3（环保）：
The plastic bag ban **exemplifies** how policy changes can reshape behavior.
```

### 方法3：语境练习（Contextual Application）

**步骤**：
1. 准备10个常见话题（教育/科技/环保等）
2. 为每个话题准备3个Band 8词汇
3. 写一段话，自然使用这些词汇

**示例（环保话题）**：
```markdown
选定词汇：mitigate/alleviate/catastrophic/indispensable

段落：
To **mitigate** the **catastrophic** effects of climate change, governments must implement comprehensive policies. Individual actions, while valuable, are **indispensable** but insufficient. Only through collective effort can we **alleviate** this pressing crisis.
```

### 方法4：对比练习（Contrastive Analysis）

**步骤**：
1. 收集自己的写作/口语答案
2. 用高亮标出所有Band 6词汇
3. 逐一替换升级
4. 对比原版和升级版

## 💡 高效记忆策略

### 策略1：搭配记忆（Collocation Learning）

不要单独记单词，而是记忆常用搭配：

| 单词 | 搭配示例 |
|:---|:---|
| **mitigate** | mitigate the problem/mitigate risk/mitigate damage |
| **exemplify** | exemplify the trend/exemplify the principle/exemplify how |
| **indispensable** | indispensable for/indispensable to/indispensable tool |
| **catalyze** | catalyze change/catalyze growth/catalyze innovation |
| **alleviate** | alleviate poverty/alleviate stress/alleviate suffering |

### 策略2：场景归类（Scenario Grouping）

按话题归类记忆：

**教育话题词库**：
- pedagogical approach / instructional method
- academic stress / learning outcome
- critical thinking / rote learning
- foster creativity / cultivate skills

**环保话题词库**：
- environmental degradation / ecological crisis
- sustainability / conservation efforts
- mitigate climate change / carbon footprint
- renewable energy / green technology

### 策略3：词族扩展（Word Family）

学习一个词，扩展整个词族：

| 词根 | 动词 | 名词 | 形容词 | 副词 |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **signify** | signify | significance | significant | significantly |
| **develop** | develop | development | developmental | - |
| **vary** | vary | variation/variety | various/varied | variously |
| **argue** | argue | argument | argumentative | argumentatively |
| **create** | create | creation/creativity | creative | creatively |
