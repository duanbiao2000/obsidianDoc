**[[百词斩生词清单]] 情境化学习与造句示例（第二批，含音标）**


*   **readiness** n 敏捷 ['redinəs]
    *   **情境/造句**: 消防队员必须时刻保持**快速反应**的能力，以便应对突发情况。
        *   *Example Sentence*: Firefighters must maintain a constant state of **readiness** to respond to emergencies.

*   **menace** v 威胁 ['menəs]
    *   **情境/造句**: 污染正在**威胁**野生动物的生存环境。
        *   *Example Sentence*: Pollution is **menacing** the habitats of wild animals.

*   **rudiment** n 初级阶段 ['ruːdɪmənt]
    *   **情境/造句**: 他只学了一点点编程的**基础知识**，还需要深入学习。
        *   *Example Sentence*: He has only learned the **rudiments** of programming and needs to study further.

*   **vicarious** adj 发同感的 [vɪ'keəriəs]
    *   **情境/造句**: 通过观看旅行纪录片，她体验到了一种**间接的**冒险乐趣。
        *   *Example Sentence*: By watching travel documentaries, she experienced a **vicarious** sense of adventure.

*   **grimy** adj 肮脏的 ['graɪmi]
    *   **情境/造句**: 经过一天的户外活动，孩子们的衣服都变得**脏兮兮的**。
        *   *Example Sentence*: After a day outside, the children's clothes were all **grimy**.

*   **solicit** v 乞求 [sə'lɪsɪt]
    *   **情境/造句**: 他向过往的行人**乞讨**零钱。
        *   *Example Sentence*: He would **solicit** spare change from passers-by.

*   **awry** adv 歪斜地 [ə'raɪ]
    *   **情境/造句**: 原本完美的计划因为一个意外而**出了差错**。
        *   *Example Sentence*: The perfectly planned event went **awry** due to an unexpected issue.

*   **lethargic** adj 困倦的 [lə'θɑːdʒɪk]
    *   **情境/造句**: 感冒让他感到非常**没有精神**，只想睡觉。
        *   *Example Sentence*: The cold made him feel very **lethargic**, all he wanted to do was sleep.

*   **conjecture** n 推测 [kən'dʒektʃə(r)]
    *   **情境/造句**: 关于她突然离职的原因，大家只是在**猜测**。
        *   *Example Sentence*: There was much **conjecture** about the reasons for her sudden departure.

*   **impervious** adj 不能渗透的 [ɪm'pɜːviəs]
    *   **情境/造句**: 这种新型材料**不透水**，非常适合做户外装备。
        *   *Example Sentence*: This new material is **impervious** to water, making it ideal for outdoor gear.

*   **verbatim** adj 逐字的 [vɜː'beɪtɪm]
    *   **情境/造句**: 记者记录下了证人**一字不差的**陈述。
        *   *Example Sentence*: The reporter wrote down the witness's statement **verbatim**.

*   **boisterous** adj 喧闹的 ['bɔɪstərəs]
    *   **情境/造句**: 孩子们在操场上**吵闹嬉戏**。
        *   *Example Sentence*: The children were **boisterous** and playing loudly in the playground.

*   **frailty** n 脆弱 ['freɪlti]
    *   **情境/造句**: 她的身体因为生病而变得非常**虚弱**。
        *   *Example Sentence*: Her physical **frailty** increased after the illness.

*   **cramp** n 痉挛 [kræmp]
    *   **情境/造句**: 跑步时，他的腿突然抽筋了，感到一阵**痉挛**。
        *   *Example Sentence*: While running, his leg suddenly seized up with a **cramp**.
