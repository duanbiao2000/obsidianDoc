# DSE 英文写作结构公式

> [!NOTE] 核心思想
> 本笔记汇总了香港 DSE 英文写作中常用的四大结构模板 (FRAI, QRS, HKABS, GMPAW)，旨在为不同文体提供清晰、高效的逻辑组织框架，帮助考生在考试中快速构建完整、连贯的论证。

## FRAI 公式：致编辑信 (Letter to the Editor)

> **用途**：专为处理正式信件类题目，尤其是 **致编辑信 (Letter to the Editor)**，确保格式正确、逻辑清晰。

### 核心结构

| 缩写 | 含义 (Meaning) | 功能说明 (Function) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **F** | **F**ormat / Greeting | 开头格式与问候语，如 “Dear Editor,” |
| **R** | **R**eason for Writing | 开宗明义，直接说明写信原因（回应文章、表达关切等） |
| **A** | **A**rguments | 主体段落，每段一个清晰论点，并辅以例子或解释 |
| **I** | **I**nvitation / Closing | 结尾呼吁读者行动或提出期望，并使用正式结束语 |

### 应用示例

> *Dear Editor,*
>
> I am writing to express my profound concern regarding the growing problem of light pollution in our city, an issue recently highlighted in your newspaper. *(F + R)*
>
> To begin with, excessive artificial light disrupts the natural ecosystem... *(A1)* Furthermore, it poses significant health risks to citizens... *(A2)*
>
> I sincerely hope that the government and your readers will take this matter seriously and support initiatives for responsible lighting. *(I)*
>
> *Yours faithfully,*
> *Chris Wong*

## QRS 公式：通用文章 (Article / Blog)

> **用途**：适用于通用文章或博客等需要吸引读者兴趣的文体，结构灵活、节奏明快。

### 核心结构

| 缩写 | 含义 (Meaning) | 功能说明 (Function) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Q** | **Q**uestion / Quote / Hook | 以一个引人入胜的问题、名言或趣事作为开头，抓住读者眼球 |
| **R** | **R**easons / Reflections | 主体部分，逐点分析主题背后的原因、现象或个人思考 |
| **S** | **S**olution / Suggestion | 结尾部分，提出解决方案、建议或对全文进行总结 |

### 应用示例

> *Have you ever wondered why, despite being more connected than ever, so many of us feel profoundly lonely?* (Q)
>
> The paradox of modern life is that digital connections often lack the depth of genuine human interaction. We scroll through endless feeds, yet miss the feeling of being truly seen and heard. (R)
>
> Perhaps it’s time to put down our phones and intentionally cultivate face-to-face relationships. The solution might be simpler than we think. (S)

## HKABS 公式：议论文 (Argumentative Essay)

> **用途**：DSE Paper 2 长篇议论文的核心框架，强调立场鲜明、论证严谨。这是 [[议论文写作]] 的经典结构。

### 核心结构

| 缩写 | 含义 (Meaning) | 功能说明 (Function) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **H** | **H**ook | 引入段，用数据、名言或争议性问题引出主题 |
| **K** | **K**ey Idea / Thesis Statement | 立场句，在引言末尾明确表达你的核心观点 |
| **A** | **A**rgument 1 | 主论点一，提供清晰的论据和例子来支持你的立场 |
| **B** | **B**ody Paragraph 2 (Argument 2) | 主论点二，从另一个角度进一步强化你的立场 |
| **S** | **S**ummary / Stand | 结论段，重申立场、总结论点并可进行适当升华 |

### 应用示例

> *In an age dominated by artificial intelligence, many fear that technology will soon render human creativity obsolete. But is this truly the case?* (H)
>
> I strongly believe that technology, rather than replacing human creativity, will serve as a powerful catalyst to augment it. (K)
>
> Firstly, AI tools can handle repetitive tasks, freeing up human creators to focus on conceptual and strategic thinking. (A) Moreover, AI can generate novel patterns and data insights that inspire new forms of art and innovation. (B)
>
> Ultimately, the future belongs not to AI alone, but to the synergy between human ingenuity and machine intelligence. (S)

## GMPAW 公式：演讲稿 (Speech)

> **用途**：专为 [[演讲稿]] 设计，强调现场感、互动性和情感号召力。

### 核心结构

| 缩写 | 含义 (Meaning) | 功能说明 (Function) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **G** | **G**reeting | 开场问候，与听众建立联系，如 “Good morning, everyone.” |
| **M** | **M**essage / Main Idea | 明确主题，告诉听众你今天演讲的核心信息 |
| **P** | **P**ersuasive Points | 主要论点，使用修辞、故事或数据来增强说服力和感染力 |
| **A** | **A**ppeal to Audience | 直接呼吁，向听众发出行动邀请或情感共鸣的挑战 |
| **W** | **W**rap-up / Ending | 收尾总结，用一句强有力的口号、名言或反问来结束，令人印象深刻 |

### 应用示例

> *Good morning, teachers and fellow students.* (G)
>
> Today, I want to talk not about our grades or our future careers, but about something far more fundamental: the power of kindness. (M)
>
> In our competitive world, we often forget that a simple act of kindness—a word of encouragement, a helping hand—can change someone's entire day. (P)
>
> So I ask you all, let’s make a conscious choice to look out for one another, to celebrate each other's successes and support each other through failures. (A)
>
> Remember: grades will fade, trophies will gather dust, but the impact of your kindness will last a lifetime. Thank you. (W)

## 四大公式速查对比

| 模板 | 适用文体 | 逻辑流向 | 写作重点 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **FRAI** | Letter to the Editor | 正式沟通 → 表达意见 | 格式严谨、观点清晰 |
| **QRS** | Article / Blog | 吸引读者 → 分析现象 → 提出见解 | 语气自然、节奏流畅 |
| **HKABS** | Argumentative Essay | 明确立场 → 逻辑支撑 → 总结升华 | 理性客观、层次分明 |
| **GMPAW** | Speech | 建立连接 → 传递信息 → 呼吁行动 | 情感真挚、互动性强 |
