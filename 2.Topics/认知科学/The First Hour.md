
### **The First Hour**

The morning is your best time.
Your mind is clear. Your energy is high.
Use this time for important work.
Work that builds your future.
Do not waste it on small things.

---

### **The Work**

Choose one thing.

*   **Think.** Plan your future.
*   **Learn.** Build a new skill.
*   **Create.** Make something valuable.
*   **Train.** Care for your body.
*   **Build.** Grow your career and your wealth.
*   **Improve.** Enrich your life.

---

### **The Rules**

Focus on the long game.
Act. Do not react to email.
Do the important work first.
Win the morning. Win the day.
