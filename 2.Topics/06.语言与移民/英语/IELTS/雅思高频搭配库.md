# 雅思高频搭配库

> **使用说明**：本库针对雅思7.5分考生整理，聚焦写作Task 2和口语Part 3高频搭配。优先掌握带★的必用搭配。

## 教育类 Education

### 名词搭配
- ★ **academic achievement** - 学术成就
  - 例句：Students should be encouraged to pursue academic achievement rather than just grades.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育话题
  - 搭配变形：high academic achievement, outstanding academic achievement

- ★ **curriculum development** - 课程开发
  - 例句：Effective curriculum development requires input from teachers, students, and industry experts.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育改革
  - 同义词：curriculum design, syllabus planning

- **educational inequality** - 教育不平等
  - 例句：The government must address educational inequality to ensure equal opportunities for all.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育公平
  - 相关词：educational disparity, academic gap

- **learning environment** - 学习环境
  - 例句：A positive learning environment significantly enhances students' academic performance.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育方法
  - 形容词搭配：conducive learning environment

- **teaching methodology** - 教学方法
  - 例句：Modern teaching methodologies should focus on student-centered approaches.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育创新
  - 同义词：teaching approach, pedagogical method

### 动词搭配
- ★ **acquire knowledge** - 获取知识
  - 例句：The primary purpose of education is to help students acquire knowledge and critical thinking skills.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育目的
  - 同义搭配：gain knowledge, obtain knowledge

- ★ **foster creativity** - 培养创造力
  - 例句：Schools should create an environment that fosters creativity and innovation.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育方法
  - 相关词：nurture creativity, stimulate creativity

- **enhance learning** - 提升学习
  - 例句：Technology can significantly enhance learning outcomes when used appropriately.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育科技
  - 搭配变形：enhance learning experience, enhance learning outcomes

- **address educational issues** - 解决教育问题
  - 例句：Policymakers must address educational issues such as funding disparities and teacher shortages.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育政策
  - 同义词：tackle educational problems

- **cultivate skills** - 培养技能
  - 例句：Education should cultivate both academic and practical skills.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育目标
  - 相关搭配：cultivate critical thinking, cultivate problem-solving skills

### 形容词搭配
- ★ **comprehensive education** - 全面教育
  - 例句：A comprehensive education system should include both academic and vocational training.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育体系
  - 相关词：holistic education, well-rounded education

- ★ **rigorous academic standards** - 严格的学术标准
  - 例句：Universities must maintain rigorous academic standards to ensure the quality of graduates.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 高等教育
  - 搭配变形：maintain rigorous standards, enforce rigorous standards

- **accessible education** - 可及教育
  - 例句：Education should be accessible to everyone regardless of their socioeconomic background.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教育公平
  - 同义词：affordable education, inclusive education

- **effective teaching methods** - 有效的教学方法
  - 例句：Teachers should employ effective teaching methods that cater to diverse learning styles.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 教学质量
  - 相关搭配：effective learning strategies, effective pedagogical approaches

- **practical skills** - 实用技能
  - 例句：Schools should equip students with practical skills for the job market.
  - 应用：Writing Task 2 - 职业教育
  - 对比词：theoretical knowledge

## 使用指南

### 每日应用方法
1. **搭配学习**：每天从对应话题选3个搭配，造句并录音
2. **话题分类**：根据当周写作话题，优先学习该话题搭配
3. **同义替换**：练习用不同搭配表达相同意思

### 写作Task 2应用
- 引言段：使用话题相关名词搭配（如 environmental degradation）
- 主体段：使用动词搭配展开论述（如 mitigate climate change）
- 结论段：使用形容词搭配总结观点（如 sustainable development）

### 口语Part 3应用
- 回答问题：使用必用搭配展现词汇广度
- 举例说明：用搭配丰富论述内容
- 观点表达：使用搭配增强表达自然度

### 难度分级
- ★ = 必用搭配（Band 7.5+必备）
- 无星 = 拓展搭配（Band 8.0+进阶）
